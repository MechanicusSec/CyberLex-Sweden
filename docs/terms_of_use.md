# CyberLex Sweden Terms of Use

## Purpose

These Terms of Use describe how CyberLex Sweden should be used.

CyberLex Sweden is an educational prototype created as a final school project. It provides simplified source-based information about selected Swedish and EU cybersecurity law topics.

CyberLex Sweden does not provide legal advice.

---

## Educational Use Only

CyberLex Sweden is intended for educational, demonstration, and portfolio purposes.

The application is designed to help users understand selected cybersecurity law topics by showing source-based answers, official source links, source metadata, and matched source excerpts.

---

## No Legal Advice

CyberLex Sweden is not a lawyer, law firm, court, public authority, or official legal service.

The information provided by CyberLex Sweden should not be treated as legal advice.

Users should verify important legal questions with:

- official legal sources
- relevant public authorities
- qualified legal professionals
- current laws and regulations

---

## Limited Scope

CyberLex Sweden only covers selected cybersecurity-related legal topics.

Current topics include:

- GDPR and personal data breaches
- IMY and Swedish GDPR supervision
- GDPR core principles
- NIS2 and the Swedish Cybersecurity Act
- NIS2 incident reporting
- Swedish cybercrime law and dataintrång
- EU attacks against information systems
- EU Cyber Resilience Act
- EU DORA and digital operational resilience

Questions outside the project scope may be refused.

---

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for how they interpret and use the information provided by CyberLex Sweden.

Users should not rely on CyberLex Sweden as the only source for legal, compliance, or cybersecurity decisions.

---

## Prohibited Use

CyberLex Sweden should not be used to:

- commit cybercrime
- plan unauthorized access
- bypass security controls
- attack systems
- avoid legal responsibility
- replace professional legal advice
- make high-risk legal decisions without verification

---

## Source-Based Answers

CyberLex Sweden attempts to answer only from local trusted knowledge base files.

The application displays citation details, official source links, source metadata, and matched source excerpts when a source is found.

If no trusted source is found, CyberLex Sweden should refuse to answer confidently.

---

## Accuracy Limitation

CyberLex Sweden may contain errors, outdated information, incomplete summaries, or technical limitations.

Cybersecurity law and digital compliance rules can change over time.

Users should always check official sources.

---

## Changes to the Project

CyberLex Sweden may be updated over time.

Future updates may include:

- more source files
- stronger search
- vector search
- language model integration
- improved citations
- public deployment
- updated legal policies
