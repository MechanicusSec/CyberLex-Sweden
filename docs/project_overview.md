# Project Overview: CyberLex Sweden

## Project Name

CyberLex Sweden

## Project Idea

CyberLex Sweden is an AI-powered assistant focused on Swedish cybersecurity law and digital compliance.

The system is designed to answer questions about cybercrime, GDPR, NIS2, incident reporting, and cybersecurity responsibilities in Sweden.

## Problem

Cybersecurity law is difficult to understand because relevant information is spread across many different places, including:

- Swedish laws
- EU regulations and directives
- Government agencies
- Legal guidance documents
- Cybersecurity authorities

This makes it hard for students, IT workers, and small organizations to quickly understand what rules apply in different situations.

## Solution

CyberLex Sweden will use AI together with trusted sources to provide clear answers in plain English.

The system should not guess. It should answer based on selected documents and official sources.

## Target Users

- IT students
- Cybersecurity students
- Small organizations
- IT support staff
- People who want to understand Swedish cybersecurity law

## Example Questions

- Is unauthorized access illegal in Sweden?
- What should a company do after a ransomware attack?
- What is the difference between GDPR and NIS2?
- When must a personal data breach be reported?
- Which Swedish authorities are involved in cybersecurity law?
- What legal responsibilities does an organization have after a cyber incident?

## Limitations

CyberLex Sweden is an educational tool and does not provide official legal advice.

The system should always recommend checking official sources or contacting a qualified legal professional for serious legal matters.
