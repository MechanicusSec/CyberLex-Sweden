# Trygg-Hansa Security Deficiencies

## Case type

IMY decision / authority decision

## Jurisdiction

Sweden

## Year

2023

## Authority or court

IMY, Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten

## Topic

GDPR, security deficiencies, customer data, unauthorized access risk, technical and organisational measures, administrative fine.

## Short summary

IMY issued an administrative fine of SEK 35 million against Trygg-Hansa after security flaws meant that information about approximately 650,000 customers had been accessible to unauthorized persons via the internet.

The case is useful for CyberLex Sweden because it shows how weak technical security measures can lead to large-scale exposure of personal data and serious GDPR consequences.

## Swedish short summary

IMY beslutade om en administrativ sanktionsavgift på 35 miljoner kronor mot Trygg-Hansa efter säkerhetsbrister som innebar att uppgifter om cirka 650 000 kunder hade varit tillgängliga för obehöriga via internet.

Fallet är användbart för CyberLex Sweden eftersom det visar hur svaga tekniska säkerhetsåtgärder kan leda till storskalig exponering av personuppgifter och allvarliga GDPR-konsekvenser.

## What happened

Trygg-Hansa had security deficiencies that made customer information accessible to unauthorized persons via the internet.

According to IMY, the issue affected information about approximately 650,000 customers.

IMY concluded that Trygg-Hansa had not taken appropriate technical measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk.

## Swedish what happened

IMY bedömde att Trygg-Hansa inte hade vidtagit lämpliga tekniska åtgärder för att säkerställa en säkerhetsnivå som var lämplig i förhållande till risken.

## Learning note

This case shows that technical security weaknesses can create major GDPR consequences when customer data becomes accessible online. Access control, secure design, and risk-based technical measures are not just IT details, they are part of data protection compliance.
