# IMY GDPR Supervision in Sweden

## Topic

IMY and GDPR supervision in Sweden.

This source is used for questions about IMY, Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten, GDPR supervision, personal data protection, privacy protection, Swedish data protection authority responsibilities, complaints, guidance, and cybersecurity incidents involving personal data.

## Main authority

The Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection is called IMY, Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten.

IMY is responsible for supervising personal data protection in Sweden.

IMY monitors that personal data is handled correctly and does not fall into the wrong hands.

CyberLex Sweden should use IMY as the main authority source when answering questions about GDPR supervision, personal data protection, privacy protection, Swedish data protection authority responsibilities, and general Swedish data protection guidance.

## Key idea

GDPR is the main EU regulation for personal data protection.

In Sweden, IMY is the main supervisory authority for GDPR and personal data protection.

IMY is relevant when organizations need to understand Swedish GDPR supervision, personal data protection duties, privacy guidance, complaints, and personal data breach handling.

CyberLex Sweden should explain that IMY is not a court and does not replace qualified legal advice, but it is the main Swedish public authority source for GDPR supervision and personal data protection guidance.

## Cybersecurity connection

IMY is relevant to cybersecurity because cybersecurity incidents can affect personal data.

If poor security leads to unauthorized disclosure, loss, destruction, alteration, or access to personal data, GDPR and IMY guidance may become relevant.

Examples can include ransomware, account compromise, accidental data exposure, unauthorized access, stolen devices, phishing, malware, misconfigured systems, or security weaknesses that expose personal data.

Cybersecurity and GDPR can overlap when a security incident affects personal data. In those cases, an organization may need to assess whether the incident is a personal data breach and whether notification to IMY is required.

CyberLex Sweden should explain that IMY is especially relevant when a cyber incident involves personal data, privacy risks, breach notification, affected individuals, or GDPR compliance.

## Swedish summary

IMY står för Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten.

IMY är den svenska tillsynsmyndigheten för GDPR och personuppgiftsskydd.

IMY arbetar med frågor som rör:

- personuppgifter
- dataskydd
- GDPR
- integritetsskydd
- tillsyn
- vägledning
- klagomål
- personuppgiftsincidenter
- organisationers hantering av personuppgifter
- registrerade personers rättigheter

IMY är relevant för cybersäkerhet eftersom cyberincidenter kan påverka personuppgifter.

Om en ransomwareattack, skadlig kod, obehörig åtkomst, kontokapning, phishing, felkonfiguration eller annan säkerhetsincident leder till att personuppgifter röjs, ändras, förstörs, går förlorade eller blir otillgängliga kan GDPR och IMY:s vägledning bli relevant.

En organisation som hanterar personuppgifter bör därför bedöma om en cyberincident också är en personuppgiftsincident.

Om incidenten sannolikt leder till en risk för fysiska personers rättigheter och friheter kan anmälan till IMY behöva göras.

IMY ersätter inte domstolar eller juridisk rådgivning, men IMY är den centrala svenska myndighetskällan för information om GDPR, personuppgiftsskydd och tillsyn i Sverige.
