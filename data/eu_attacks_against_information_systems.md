# EU Attacks Against Information Systems Directive

## Topic

EU cybercrime rules related to attacks against information systems.

## Main authority

The official EU legal source is Directive 2013/40/EU on attacks against information systems.

## Key idea

Directive 2013/40/EU concerns attacks against information systems.

It establishes minimum rules concerning criminal offences and sanctions in the area of cybercrime.

## Important points

The directive is relevant to cybercrime topics such as:

- illegal access to information systems
- illegal system interference
- illegal data interference
- tools used for cybercrime
- cooperation between competent authorities
