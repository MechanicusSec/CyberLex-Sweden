# GDPR Core Principles

## Topic

General Data Protection Regulation, GDPR, core principles and responsibilities.

## Main authority

In Sweden, GDPR and personal data protection are supervised by IMY, Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten.

GDPR itself is an EU regulation. The official legal text is Regulation (EU) 2016/679.

## Key idea

GDPR regulates how personal data may be processed.

The regulation protects natural persons when their personal data is handled by organizations, companies, authorities, and other controllers.

## Important points

GDPR includes principles such as:

- lawfulness, fairness, and transparency
- purpose limitation
- data minimisation
- accuracy
- storage limitation
- integrity and confidentiality
- accountability

Organizations that process personal data must have a legal basis and must protect the personal data they handle.

## Cybersecurity connection

GDPR is relevant to cybersecurity because poor security can lead to personal data breaches.

Organizations need appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data.
